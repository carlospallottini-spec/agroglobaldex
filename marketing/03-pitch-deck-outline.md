## Slide 2 — The Problem

**Título:** Agro tokenization is fragmented, illiquid, and regulatorily fragile.

- Producers and originators (Agrotoken, Topaz, Moss, Toucan, etc.) issue tokens — but each lives in a silo with limited global buyers.
- Carbon credits and grain tokens are spread across 10+ chains and 30+ platforms, with no neutral aggregator.
- Most issuers are LATAM/US-based and lack a clear, MiCA-compliant path to European institutional capital.

---

## Slide 3 — Why now

**Título:** Three tailwinds converging in 2026.

- **Regulatory clarity:** MiCA in force since Dec 2024 — first time crypto-assets backed by RWA have a clear EU framework.
- **CBAM ramp-up:** EU carbon border tax phasing in — driving institutional demand for verifiable agro carbon credits.
- **RWA momentum:** Tokenized real-world assets crossed USD 30B+ on-chain [VERIFICAR cifra rwa.xyz] — agro is the next vertical after Treasuries.

---

## Slide 4 — The Solution

**Título:** A vertical, chain-neutral marketplace for tokenized agriculture.

- We aggregate tokens issued by 3rd parties (Agrotoken, Topaz, Toucan, Nori, etc.) — we do NOT compete with them.
- Single, MiCA-compliant venue for EU buyers (institutional and accredited retail).
- Settlement on Solana; cross-chain custody via Pyth/Chainlink. Issuers keep their chain of origin.

---

## Slide 5 — How it works

**Título:** Three sides of the market.

- **Originators (LATAM/US):** producers and platforms that already tokenize. We onboard them and provide global distribution.
- **Buyers (EU/global):** institutional investors, treasuries, corporates needing CBAM-compliant carbon, traders.
- **Infra:** Pyth (price feeds), Chainlink (cross-chain), regulated custody partner, MiCA legal wrapper.
